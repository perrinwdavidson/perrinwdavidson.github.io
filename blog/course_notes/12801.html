12.801: Large-Scale Ocean Circulation

Syllabus

The list of topics covered in this course are as follows:

  1. Geostrophic dynamics
  2. Planetary geostrophy
  3. Ekman pumping
  4. Wind and thermally driven ocean circulation
  5. Thermocline theory
  6. Western-boundary current dynamics
  7. Abyssal circulation
  8. Mixing
  9. Dynamics of circumpolar jets
  10. Baroclinic instability
  11. Geostrophic turbulence
  12. Eddy-mean flow interaction

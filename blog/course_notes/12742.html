12.742: Marine Chemistry

Syllabus

The list of topics covered in this course are as follows:

  1. History of oceanography
  2. Composition of seawater
  3. Mass balance
  4. Box models
  5. Climate-ocean System
  6. Ocean circulation
  7. Water masses
  8. Surface ocean boundary conditions
  9. Primary production
  10. Export and new production
  11. Remineralization
  12. Carbon Cycle
  13. Oxygen Minimum Zones
  14. Seawater thermodynamics
  15. Carbonate system
  16. Gas exchange
  17. Isotope geochemistry
  18. Continental weathering
  19. Estuaries and vents
  20. Sediments
  21. Carbon carbonate compensation
  22. Ice ages
  23. Anthropocene

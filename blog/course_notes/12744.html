12.744: Marine Isotope Chemistry

Syllabus

The list of topics covered in this course are as follows:

  1. Chart of the nuclides
  2. Stallar nucleosynthesis
  3. Mass spectrometry
  4. Mass-dependent fractionation
  5. Instrumental mass bias
  6. Stable isotope systematics
  7. Water cycle
  8. CHONS
  9. Clumped isotopes
  10. Box model perturbations
  11. Cosmogenic nuclides
  12. Radiocarbon
  13. Non-traditional light stable isotopes
  14. Radiometric dating
  15. Earth differentiation
  16. Decay series

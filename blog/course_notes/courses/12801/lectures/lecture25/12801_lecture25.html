12.801: Lecture 25

ACC and Residual Mean Theory

The Setup

We have that on $f$-contours, the Taylor columns do not move north or south (i.e., the vortex stretching term remains constant). Furthermore, we have that the ocean gyres away from the Antarctic Circumpolar Current (ACC) are in Sverdup balance. However, in the ACC it is not in Sverdup balance as the zonal pressure gradient vanishes when you integrate across the path length of the ACC, or a north-south geostrophic flow.

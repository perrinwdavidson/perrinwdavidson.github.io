Summer 2022 Work Journal

Week 1 (21/06 to 24/06)

Goals

The goals of this week were to:

  1. calculate and plot sea-ice fraction (sic) and surface wind (w) speed differences between the Last Glacial Maximum (LGM) and Pre-Industrial (PI) for individual models & the inter-model mean
  2. read four papers, focusing on two themes:
    • disequilibrium mechanisms, comparing Hamme (2017) to Loose (2016)
    • gas exchange parameterizations, comparing Nicholson (2011, 2016) to Liang (2013)

Forcing Comparisons

I first determined which products from the Coupled Model Intercomparison Project 5 (CMIP5) outputted the two variables that we care about: the sic and the w. In order to have a consistent comparison across both the LGM and PI, I only used those model products that outputted both variables. This left 7 models:
